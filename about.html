About Joerg Proeve

Founder & Principal Risk Advisor

I help MSPs close enterprise deals and survive claims.

Built from the Carrier Side

Most brokers have only ever sold insurance. An engineer by training, I started my career in cybersecurity before spending two decades inside the insurance industry, first at Chubb and CNA, then I founded a parametric InsurTech startup and helped build a cyber MGA. I have sat in the rooms where carriers decide what gets covered and what gets excluded.

I started Breezy Risk Advisors because the businesses that need the most sophisticated coverage analysis are getting the least.

View my LinkedIn profile →

My background changes how I read your policies. I understand the technology you deliver, so I can spot where policy language does not match what your team does. I am not scanning for checkboxes. I am looking for the same ambiguities and exclusion triggers that a carrier's claims team will use to deny a payout. When I flag a gap, it is because I have seen that exact scenario play out from the inside.

How We Work Together

I work with managed service providers that have outgrown their broker's understanding: MSPs adding service lines, closing enterprise contracts, acquiring firms, or serving regulated verticals like healthcare and finance.

I also work with community banks and credit unions where overlapping cyber, fidelity bond, and D&O policies create coverage confusion that only surfaces at claim time.

The Risk Intelligence Report is a detailed review of your current policies to identify gaps and exclusions. No sales pitch. Just an honest assessment of where you stand and what needs fixing.
