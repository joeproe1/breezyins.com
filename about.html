About Joerg Proeve

Founder & Principal Risk Advisor

I help MSPs understand what their insurance actually covers, before a claim or contract review exposes the gaps.

Why I Started Breezy Risk Advisors

Insurance for MSPs is broken. Most brokers don't understand the technology they're supposed to protect. I spent 20+ years at Chubb, CNA, and building cyber insurance programs, and I saw this gap everywhere.

My background is different. I started in cybersecurity at Siemens, working with the infrastructure and systems that MSPs manage and protect. This technical foundation gives me a dual perspective: I understand the risks MSPs face and the insurance products that claim to cover them.

I founded Breezy Risk Advisors because MSPs were getting sold policies that don't cover what they actually do. When I review your insurance, I'm not checking boxes. I'm looking at real scenarios: ransomware response, failed migrations, client lawsuits. I know where the gaps hide.

What Makes My Approach Different

I don't work like traditional brokers. I start by understanding what you actually do, not what a policy template says you do. Then I show you exactly where coverage will respond, where it won't, and what it will cost to fix.

I speak your language, no insurance jargon, no sales tactics. Just honest answers: Will your policy cover a ransomware incident tied to your remote access? What happens if a cloud migration goes wrong? Does your coverage meet that enterprise contract you're trying to close?
