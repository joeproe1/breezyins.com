WHERE BANK INSURANCE BREAKS DOWN

SCENARIO 01 · WIRE FRAUD

"Is that a cyber claim or a bond claim?"

An employee clicks a spoofed email. A wire transfer goes out. Your cyber carrier says social engineering falls under the fidelity bond. Your bond carrier says computer fraud falls under cyber.

You are stuck in the middle.

SCENARIO 02 · BOARD LIABILITY

"The board got sued after the breach"

Regulators investigate board oversight of cybersecurity. Your D&O excludes claims "arising from" a cyber event. Your cyber policy does not cover board defense costs.

Nobody pays for the defense.

SCENARIO 03 · SECURITY WARRANTY

"We had coverage. We thought."

Your cyber policy requires MFA on all remote access. Your core banking vendor does not support it. After a breach, the carrier rescinds coverage on a warranty you did not know you were making.

Coverage voided at the worst moment.
