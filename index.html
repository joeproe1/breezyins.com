Three Ways MSP Insurance Fails

"Lost the enterprise deal"

Fortune 500 prospect requires proof of $5M cyber coverage. Your policy tops out at $2M.
Deal dies in procurement.

"Sued after a ransomware incident"

Client's network encrypted after your remote access was compromised. Your carrier says it's a cyber claim. Cyber carrier says it's a professional services claim.
You're stuck with legal bills.

"Broker sold you the wrong coverage"

You told your broker you do pen testing and incident response. They quoted 'IT consulting' and excluded security services.
You find out when you file a claim.

These coverage gaps hide in policy language and surface during contract reviews or claims. By then it's too late to fix them.
