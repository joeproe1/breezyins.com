What you get

Coverage Gap Analysis

Line-by-line check of your insurance policies against the services you actually deliver: managed services, security assessments, pen testing, incident response, cloud migrations.

Contract Compliance Review

Review of up to 3 client agreements (MSAs/SOWs) to flag where limits, deductibles, cancellation notice, or required wording don't match.

Practical claim-readiness

Real-world scenarios showing what happens when you file a claim: ransomware incidents, client lawsuits, data breaches, and whether your policy will actually pay out.

Fixes you can act on

Clear recommendations on what to change, why it matters, and what to ask your broker to update.

Designed for MSPs with up to ~50 employees, who want clear answers without months of process or a large consulting bill.
